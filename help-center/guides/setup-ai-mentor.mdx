## Overview
Libro’s AIメンター helps you practice and get feedback instantly.

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable AI Mentor">
    Go to **Settings** > **Mentor** <br/>
    Toggle **AI Mentor** ON.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a session">
    Click **New Lesson**
    <br/>
    Choose **With AI Mentor** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interact and learn">
    Ask questions, complete tasks, and receive instant feedback as you learn.
  </Step>
</Steps>
