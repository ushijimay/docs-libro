title: How do I reset my progress?
description: Reset your learning progress in Libro.
---

## Overview

You can reset your study progress to start fresh anytime.

## Steps

1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click on **Profile**
3. Select **Progress Settings**
4. Click **Reset Progress**
5. Confirm action in the dialog

<Callout kind="warning">
Resetting will delete all progress data. Action cannot be undone.
</Callout>
