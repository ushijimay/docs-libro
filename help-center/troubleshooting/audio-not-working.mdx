---
title: Audio Not Working
description: Fix when audio does not play in Libro.
---

## Symptoms
- No sound during lessons
- AI mentor's voice not playing

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check device volume">
    Ensure your headphones or speakers are not muted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow browser audio">
    In browser settings, make sure audio is allowed for `libro.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload the page">
    Restart your browser or refresh the lesson page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
